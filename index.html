The problem

By the time you hear they raised, the budget's already spent.

A funding round is the loudest "we have budget and we're about to buy" signal in B2B. But finding those companies the moment it matters is a grind — and the window slams shut fast.

🐌

You find out too late

By the time a raise hits your feed, other vendors have already booked the call. Funded firms shortlist vendors fast — often within the first quarter.

Enterprise intent is overkill

Yes by design. RoundSignal delivers company-level signals and the role to contact — not a database of personal emails. You source the individual contact yourself, lawfully, once you've decided the account is worth it.

Those give you a raw firehose to filter and interpret yourself. RoundSignal does the filtering and the thinking — scoring each account and writing the why-now, the contact role, and the angle — so you act instead of research.
