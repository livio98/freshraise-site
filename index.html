Questions, answered.

Where does the data come from? ⌄

Public funding news — TechCrunch, Crunchbase News, EU-Startups, Tech.eu, Finsmes and 50+ others, plus targeted public news queries for your vertical. We read, dedupe, and interpret it; we don't scrape private data.

Is this GDPR-compliant? ⌄

Yes by design. FreshRaise delivers company-level signals and the role to contact — not a database of personal emails. You source the individual contact yourself, lawfully, once you've decided the account is worth it.

How is this different from Crunchbase or Apollo? ⌄

Those give you a raw firehose to filter and interpret yourself. FreshRaise does the filtering and the thinking — scoring each account and writing the why-now, the contact role, and the angle — so you act instead of research.

How fresh are the signals? ⌄

Each Monday's issue covers raises announced in the prior days — landing you well inside the 48-hour-to-90-day window where funded firms choose vendors.
